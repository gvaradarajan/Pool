Junior Pool

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Click on the cue ball and hold
  2. Drag left and right to change the angle. Drag closer and farther away from the ball to increase or decrease the force on the ball respectively.
  3. When you're ready to hit the ball, release the mouse button
  4. When the balls have stopped moving, the "READY" signal will appear and you can take another turn
  5. The game ends when you've gotten all the balls in the pockets or you scratch
